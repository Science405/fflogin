Tournament Details

Everything you need to know about the Free Fire Esports Tournament in Nepal

Tournament Date

15th Ashad, 2081

Registration closes: 10th Ashad, 2081

Team Size

4 players + 1 substitute (optional)

Individual registrations also accepted

Format

Qualifiers Round → Quaters finals→ Semi-Finals → Grand Finals

Prizes

1st Place: NPR 50,000 + Tropies
2nd Place: NPR 25,000 + Tropies
3rd Place: NPR 15,000 + Tropies

Tournament Rules

  • All players must have a valid Free Fire account
  • Teams must consist of 4 main players (substitute optional)
  • No cheating or use of unauthorized software
  • Players must be residents of Nepal
  • All matches will be monitored by tournament officials
  • Decisions by tournament organizers are final
